How to make a meat sandwich:
1. Take out ingredients: meat, mayonnaise, lettuce, tomato, bread
2. Take materials out: knife, plate
3. Slice tomatoes
4. Separate lettuce into desired amount of pieces
5. Take out 2 bread slices and place on the place
6. Open mayonnaise jar
7. Spread the mayonnaise on both slices of bread
8. Close mayonnaise jar
9. Place meat on one bread slice
10. Layer tomatoes on bread slice with meat
11. Place lettuce on the slice of bread with only mayonnaise
12. Put bread slices together
13. Put ingredients and materials away
