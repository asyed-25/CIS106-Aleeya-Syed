Chapter 2 Problem 1 (Psuedocode)
Program to prompt the user for hours and rate per hour and then calculate and display their weekly, monthly, and annual gross pay(hours * rate). 
Base monthly and annual calculations on 12 months per year and 52 weeks per year.


Have user input rate/hour and number of hours
Define variables for hour and rate/hour through user input
weekly = hours * rate * 5
monthly = hours * rate * 12
annual = hours * rate * 52
Output weekly, monthly, and annual gross pay
